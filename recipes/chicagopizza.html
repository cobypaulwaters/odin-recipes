The Perfect Chicago Style Pizza Recipe

Discover the secrets to making an authentic Chicago-style deep-dish pizza at home. This recipe features a buttery crust, rich tomato sauce, and a generous amount of cheese and toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • ½ cup melted butter
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 2 cups pizza sauce
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, cornmeal, and salt. In a separate bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Add melted butter to the yeast mixture, then combine with the dry ingredients. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic.
  3. Press the dough into a deep-dish pizza pan, making sure to cover the bottom and sides evenly.
  4. Layer the mozzarella cheese, pepperoni, and pizza sauce over the crust.
  5. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 425°F (220°C) for 25-30 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
