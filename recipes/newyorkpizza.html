The Classic New York Style Pizza Recipe

Discover the secrets to making an authentic New York-style pizza at home. This recipe features a thin, crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, and a perfect blend of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • ½ cup melted butter
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 2 cups pizza sauce
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
